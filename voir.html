About

I created a fluid layout streaming media webpage to create Voir. I embedded a video while creating a webpage dedicated to one particular show. The moodboard was for inspiration from other streaming websites such as Netflix, Hulu, HBO Max, and more which helped inspire my sketches and mockup design.
