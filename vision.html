RESEARCH AND APPLICATION

"According to Nir Eyal, author of Hooked, users need incentives to use a product. This means creating a product with the hooked cycle: trigger, action, variable reward, and investment."
TRIGGER

Triggers are cues that prompt users to take action. There are two types of triggers:

External Triggers

These come from the environment and can be advertising, notifications, or other prompts that remind users to use a product.

Internal Triggers

These are generated internally, often due to emotions, thoughts, or routines. Users might turn to a product in response to a feeling or need.

ACTION

To promise a reward to consumers. This is the behavior that the user takes in response to the trigger.

INVESTMENT

Users need to have a personal contribution. Its been shown that we value our own work more than its actually worth. This means a consumerâ€™s investment in a product increases its value disproportionately. This is called the IKEA effect

VARIABLE REWARDS

After taking the action, the user gains something useful. Rewards need to be a variable in order to hook a consumer into a cycle and form a habit. Otherwise the consumer starts anticipating the reward and will start to lose interest and wont come back to the product.

THE INCENTIVES

Camera Function with Room Dimensions

The camera function allows users to capture room dimensions accurately, enabling precise placement of furniture, decorations, and other elements within the space. This ensures that virtual design elements align seamlessly with the actual physical dimensions of the room.

Private User Profiles

User profiles within the app remain private and are only visible to the account holder. While the app isn't primarily a social media platform, it provides users with the option to explore designs created by other users, offering inspiration for their own creative projects.

Your Designs

This is the repository where all user designs will be stored.

Color Coordinator

Users will have the capability to establish folders for crafting individual room color schemes, ensuring cohesive color coordination and contrasts.

Share & Get Inspired

These features enable users to showcase their design creations to other app users, fostering a community of designers. Users can explore and draw inspiration from the work of others, stimulating creativity and encouraging collaboration within the app's community.
