ForHealth

About

ForHealth is a healthcare app that is designed to promote greater freedom and flexibility in personal health care choices. This mobile app is a free and accessible way to centralize and monitor the entire familyâ€™s health care schedule.

User Overview

Targeted Users

  • Struggling to find high quality, affordable healthcare
  • Those in underprivileged communities with limited access to healthcare
  • Elderly who need a one stop shop for ease of mind
  • Deaf and audibly impaired

Persona & Tasks

User Persona: Sarah

Task List Summary

Participants were asked to complete the following tasks on the ForHealth app:

  • Booking an appointment
  • Cancel an existing appointment
  • Check appointment details
  • Finding a physician or telehealth expert

Research Overview

Research

  • Research studies were held in the participants home between the dates of November 15th-19th
  • 20 Minute 1 on 1 sessions held with one member of the research team
  • Prototypes were executed using researcher provided Apple or Android mobile phone
  • After each task, participants indicated their perceived ease of use using a 5-point Likert scale
  • Each task began at the home page

Participant Overview

The study had 4 participants with ages ranging ages 22-53. There were 3 Females and 1 Male

Task Difficulty

Here are some of the task difficulty:

  • Task 4, finding a new doctor, was identified as the most difficult task with an average difficulty rating of 2.25.
  • Task 3, checking appointment details, was identified as the least difficult task with an average difficulty rating of 1.25.

Key Takeaways

Some keytakeaways from the ForHealth Prototype:

  • Users found our product very useful with our first prototype receiving mid to high confidence ratings
  • We transformed reviews into changes that were implemented into our final prototype
  • This app will prove to be vital for users with disabilities, as well as those from within every walk of life
