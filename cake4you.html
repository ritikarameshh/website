    About

    Cake4You is a responsive strawberry cupcake recipe page. I created this to get a hands-on experience using media queries. I, first, began with a Moodboard. Looked up many recipe pages to inspire my design. Then, I created a few sketeches to get a better understanding for what I needed for my recipe page. Next, my goal was to create a mockup of my design for three seperate devices- mobile (320px), tablet (768px), and desktop (1024px). I also created a recipe print mockup to make it easy for users to print out the recipe in a user-friendly way. After the mockups were created, I used HTML5 and CSS3 to turn my mockups into reality!
