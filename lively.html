OVERVIEW

ABOUT

Lively is the all-in-one app that puts your medical records in your pocket, allowing you to securely access, manage, and share your information anywhere, anytime. With features like appointment bookings, secure messaging with providers, and tailored provider searches, Lively streamlines your healthcare journey.

THE CHALLENGE

In today's complex healthcare landscape, accessing and managing medical records across providers and locations is a daunting task. Moving cities, seeking specialized care, or simply coordinating multiple aspects of healthcare often leads to fragmented information and barriers in obtaining comprehensive records. Lively steps in as the answer to this challenge, streamlining the process by centralizing your medical history in a secure app.

KEY OBJECTIVES

User Accessibility and Convenience

Ensure seamless access to medical records, appointment bookings, test results, and communication with healthcare providers anytime, anywhere. Prioritize user-friendly interfaces for all age groups and technical abilities.

Comprehensive Medical Record Integration

Enable users to securely store, organize, and access all medical records, including doctor notes, prescriptions, test results, and imaging scans within the app.

Interoperability and Data Portability

Implement standards to allow easy transfer and integration of medical records from various healthcare providers and systems, ensuring continuity of care if users switch providers or move to different locations.

Secure Communication Channel

Facilitate secure messaging between users and their healthcare providers, ensuring confidentiality and compliance with privacy regulations (such as HIPAA) to discuss medical concerns or seek advice.

Appointment Management

Provide a feature-rich appointment scheduling system allowing users to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments with their healthcare providers, with reminders and notifications for upcoming appointments.

Customized Provider Search and Matchmaking

Offer a search function that allows users to find healthcare providers, including specialists, based on location, expertise, patient reviews, and insurance coverage to cater to their specific healthcare needs.

Real-time Updates and Notifications

Keep users informed with timely updates about new test results, appointment reminders, medication refills, and any changes or additions to their medical records.

Integration of Wearable Devices and Health Trackers

Enable synchronization with wearable devices and health trackers to incorporate additional health data, providing a more comprehensive overview of the user's health status.

Education and Health Information Sharing

Provide educational resources and health information to empower users to better understand their conditions, treatments, and preventive care measures.

Continuous Improvement and User Feedback

Implement mechanisms to gather user feedback regularly, allowing for continuous improvement of the app's features, usability, and overall user experience.
