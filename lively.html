OVERVIEW

LIVELY REINVENTS THE MEDICAL FILING SYSTEM

Lively is the all-in-one app that puts your medical records in your pocket, allowing you to securely access, manage, and share your information anywhere, anytime. With features like appointment bookings, secure messaging with providers, and tailored provider searches, Lively streamlines your healthcare journey.

GAPS IN HEALTHCARE

In today's complex healthcare landscape, accessing and managing medical records across providers and locations is a daunting task. Moving cities, seeking specialized care, or simply coordinating multiple aspects of healthcare often leads to misinformation and barriers in collecting detailed records. I hope Lively can be the first step to solving this issue.

SELF-SERVICE CHALLENGE

1. An app to keep your medical records in one place.

2. An app to communicate with ALL healthcare providers.

3. Easy to use app, for people of all ages, for scheduling appointments .

USER RESEARCH

KEY OBJECTIVES

During our discovery phase I wanted to forecast what assumptions were correct and what else we could find to create the best healthcare service.

Quantitative Research

I sent out user surveys as they helped to determine the target audience and gain a basic understanding of what users seek for when they think of their "perfect" healthcare app. In this case, we received about 23 responses and based on those responses, I have gained some key insights:

  1. Target audiences are between 27 - 55 of age.
  2. Users with chronic illness worry more about their medical records being lost than people with regular annual check-ups.
  3. Users want an app that helps with notifying when their next appointment is, including their blood tests and scans.
